Our Story

From Passion to Purpose

Yard Guard Landscaping was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves a beautiful outdoor space they can actually enjoy. After graduating from Humber College's Horticulture Science and Landscape Design program, I spent years perfecting the craft of landscape transformation.

Today, we've helped over 500 families in Mississauga and the GTA reclaim their weekends and transform their yards into low-maintenance outdoor oases. Our team combines technical expertise with genuine care for every project we take on.

When we introduced 3D design visualization to our process, everything changed. Now our clients can see exactly what their yard will look like before we break ground - no surprises, no guesswork, just confidence in their investment.
